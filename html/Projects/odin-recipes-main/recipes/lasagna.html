Nachy's Criollo Lasagna

Description

A glorious magnificent bowl of fat thick cheese and a pound of ground beef swimming in its own grease all mounted up together to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Cheese
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cream cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the beef on a pan for 30 min on medium
  2. boil the Lasagna Noodles as described in the box
  3. slap the cooked noodles like its a floor for the beef
  4. slam the sauce on top of the noodles
  5. dunk the beef on top of the noodles
  6. make it rain the cheese on top
  7. slap another Noodle on top of that masterpiece
  8. repeat from step 3 until you think its too much
